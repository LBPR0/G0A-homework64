GOA

Goal-Oriented Academy (GOA) is a Georgian institution founded by Nika Keshelava, focused on programming. They offer personalized online programming courses for both web development and game development.

Students in GOA learn programming and leadership skills from top mentors, gaining lots of knowledge and skills.









Course Duration

The basic program lasts one year, with an in-depth course extending to 2.5 years, depending on the student's interests.









Career Opportunities

Successful students have the chance to be employed at GOA and partner companies.













GOA maintains an active presence on social media platforms, sharing updates, student achievements, and motivational content.
